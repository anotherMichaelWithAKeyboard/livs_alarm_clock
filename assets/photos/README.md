# Photo Frame Photos

Place your photos in this directory for photo frame mode.

## Supported Formats
- JPEG (`.jpg`, `.jpeg`)
- PNG (`.png`)
- BMP (`.bmp`)
- GIF (`.gif`)

## Photo Frame Mode
The alarm clock will automatically enter photo frame mode after 60 seconds of inactivity (configurable in settings).

Photos will rotate every 10 seconds, displaying in a random order.

## Tips
- Use landscape orientation photos for best fit on the 800x480 display
- Photos will be automatically scaled to fit while maintaining aspect ratio
- Add personal photos, nature scenes, or anything you'd like to see!
